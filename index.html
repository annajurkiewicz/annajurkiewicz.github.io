Anna Jurkiewicz design review

Pick a direction.

Three things to look at: two mood boards for choosing colours and type, and a first full draft of the site built from everything we have agreed so far.

Mood board 1

Characterful palettes

Three flat palettes (Karst, Flowstone, Springtail) and three expressive font pairings. Light / dark, phone preview.
Mood board 2

Compact + complex

Compact type, minimal tags, flat layout, plus gradient / iridescent palettes that shift on scroll, and parallax vs static sketches.
Mood board 3

Experimental textures

One paper / ink palette, character from the system: cave-survey, risograph, 1-bit dither, specimen, brutalist. Texture over colour.
Mood board 4

Interaction experiments

Combinable: torch-beam reveal, springtail critters, deep-time descent, and dissolving type. Move the cursor and scroll.
Full draft

The site, working

Landing, the menu pages, Works filter, Timeline and project pages. Built static so it deploys by upload.

This page is a private review hub (set to noindex). The full draft currently uses the Karst palette and the Space Grotesk + Inter Tight pairing as a default; once you choose from the mood boards, that becomes the locked theme.
