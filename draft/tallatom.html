Voice & Performance

Tallatom

a multidisciplinary performance by and with Maryam Farhang, Anna Jurkiewicz and Eva Ursprung. Initiated in 2025 together with Małgorzata Goździk.

2025 – 2026  ·  Tallatom, Farsi: turbulence

voice / performance / water / collaboration

Three artists transform the Badehaus into an intimate space through stories, poetry, sound, light and movement, open to celebrating the sensation of water on the skin and to a collective exploration of the emotional turbulences that emerge in dreams of dark waters.

If possible, please bring a bathrobe and/or a towel.
