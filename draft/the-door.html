Voice & Performance

The Door

solo voice performance · Nagelova Propast Abyss, 7 Festival, Brno / Blansko, Czech Republic

2024

voice / performance / solo / karst / deep-time

An ambulatory voice performance in the natural underground spaces of Moravský kras ties the bare, natural, open voice, singing echoes of peasant memory embodied in ritual songs, with the physical ecopoetics of rock formations and karst landscape movements.

The content of the composition directly dialogues with the environmental processes that shape (or simply are) the karst: sediments of non-human and human memory are juxtaposed, exploring the framework of deep time and the vulnerability of all organic life.
